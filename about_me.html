Hrithik Bhardwaj

I am a UK-based graduate in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (2025–26) from the University of Liverpool, a founding member of the prestigious Russell Group of research-intensive universities, ranked among the top 150 worldwide and top 25 in the UK by leading university guides.

My academic training included core modules such as Machine Learning, Big Data Analytics, Statistics for Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Programming with Python, and Data Visualization, equipping me with both theoretical foundations and practical problem-solving skills.

I enjoy turning raw data into insights using tools like Python, SQL, Power BI, Git, and GitHub, and building dynamic Power BI dashboards to present findings clearly and interactively. My hands-on project work spans machine learning models, classification systems, recommendation engines, and analytics applications that demonstrate real-world impact.

I’ve also gained professional experience working with Salesforce CRM for clients such as Nokia and CloudE Genesis, which enhanced my ability to manage data flows and support business processes. I am passionate about continuous learning and applying data science and analytics to solve meaningful problems and drive informed decision-making.
